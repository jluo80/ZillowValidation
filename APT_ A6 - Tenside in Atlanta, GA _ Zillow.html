Each cable ready apartment includes air conditioning, controlled building access, a fully equipped kitchen including dishwasher, stainless steel appliances, side by side refrigerator with filtered in-door water and ice, garbage disposal as well as patio/balconies and washer/dryer connections. Some furnished washer and dryers available.
