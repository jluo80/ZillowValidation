Tenside Apartments in Atlanta, GA offer one, two and three bedroom apartment homes for rent. Each cable ready apartment includes air conditioning, controlled building access, a fully equipped kitchen including dishwasher, stainless steel appliances, side by side refrigerator with filtered in-door water and ice, garbage disposal as well as patio/balconies and washer/dryer connections. Some furnished washer and dryers available. Designer details include 9 foot ceilings, soaking tubs, tiled shower and tubs, custom lighting package with pendant lighting, premium granite counter tops and custom cabinetry. Tenside Apartments offers outstanding amenities such as saltwater swimming pool, 24-hour athletic club, gaming room with free Wifi service, foosball, flat screen TV's, shuffle board, and Cyber cafe. Tenside also offers 24 hour emergency maintenance and professional on-site management. This pet friendly community allows large dogs up to 75lbs. Each apartment home has elevator access and is prewired for alarm systems and high speed internet access. Enjoy free covered controlled access parking, direct access to the Midtown MARTA station, easy access to US 75/85, I 20, just minutes from GA Tech and Downtown Atlanta and within walking distance to Atlanta's trendy Westside featuring restaurants, galleries and shops. Come see for yourself why the Tenside apartments make for great college and family housing.…  More Less
